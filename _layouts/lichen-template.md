## Description

*Genus species* is recognized by:

- **Thallus:** color, texture  
- **Growth form:** foliose / fruticose / crustose  
- **Lobes:** width, shape, margins  
- **Surface features:** soredia / isidia / pseudocyphellae (as applicable)  
- **Underside:** color, rhizines  
- **Attachment:** loose / tight  

General growth pattern and substrate preferences.
