Celithemis eponina

The Halloween Pennant is a small dragonfly known for its distinctive orange and black wings, which resemble a festive pennant. It is often seen fluttering in open fields, meadows, and near water during late summer and early fall. In these photos, the Halloween Pennant is shown perched on a twig, allowing for a close view of its vibrant wing pattern and delicate body structure.

For identification details and comparison with similar species, see Celithemis eponina in the Field Notes section.
