Pachydiplax longipennis

The Blue Dasher is a small dragonfly with a bright blue body and clear wings. It is commonly found near ponds, marshes, and wetlands, where it perches on reeds and other vegetation. In these photos, the Blue Dasher is shown resting on a seed head, allowing for a close view of its vibrant coloration and delicate wing structure.

For identification details and comparison with similar species, see Pachydiplax longipennis in the Field Notes section.
