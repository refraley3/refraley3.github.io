Dragonflies are agile fliers and skilled hunters, often found near water where they lay their eggs. Their vibrant colors and intricate wing patterns make them a favorite subject for insect photography. In this gallery, you can explore various species of dragonflies, each captured in moments of flight or rest, showcasing their unique beauty and behavior.

Dragonfly Galleries

This section gathers dragonfly encounters by species and field setting, from skimmers darting over ponds to meadowhawks perched on grasses. Each gallery focuses on posture, behavior, habitat, and the visual details that make these insects worth a closer look.
