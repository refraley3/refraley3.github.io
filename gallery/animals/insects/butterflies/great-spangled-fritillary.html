Speyeria cybele

The Great Spangled Fritillary is a large butterfly with bright orange wings marked by black spots and lines. The underside of the wings features a pattern of silver spots on a brown background, which can be seen when the butterfly rests with its wings closed. In these photos, the butterfly is perched description, showcasing both the vibrant upper wing pattern and the intricate details of the underside.

For identification details and comparison with similar species, see Speyeria cybelein the Field Notes section.
