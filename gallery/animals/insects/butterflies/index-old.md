<section class="animals-page">

  <p class="text-center">
    Butterflies bring color, movement, and small moments of stillness to the field: wings opening in sunlight, skippers tucked low on leaves, and brief flashes of pattern along trails, flowers, and woodland edges.
  </p>

  <h2>Butterfly Galleries</h2>

  <p>
    This section gathers butterfly encounters by species and field setting, from small skippers resting on foliage to larger, more familiar butterflies moving through meadows, gardens, and woodland openings.
    Each gallery focuses on posture, behavior, habitat, and the visual details that make these insects worth a closer look.
  </p>
