Limenitis arthemis

The Red-spotted Purple is a dark, iridescent butterfly with deep blue scaling along the hindwings and small red-orange spots near the wing margins. Although it lacks the long tails of dark swallowtails, its rich blue-black pattern can give it a similar impression at first glance. In these photos, the butterfly is shown both on a gravelly path and tucked into low green vegetation, where the blue markings stand out against the shaded wings.

For identification details and comparison with similar species, see Limenitis arthemis in the Field Notes section.
