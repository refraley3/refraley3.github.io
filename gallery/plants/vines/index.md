### Climbing Forms of the Forest

---

Vines trace the edges between forest and field — linking ground to canopy, stone to sky.  
They climb by tendrils, coils, or rootlets, finding light where trees once stood.  
Some blaze with color in autumn; others fade quietly into the understory, holding the forest together by threads.
