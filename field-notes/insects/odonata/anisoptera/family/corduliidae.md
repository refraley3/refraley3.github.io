**Corduliidae** (emeralds) are medium-sized dragonflies, frequently showing metallic or iridescent green coloration on the thorax and eyes. They are agile hunters, often seen flying swiftly over water and open areas.

### Field marks
- Metallic or iridescent green sheen on thorax and sometimes eyes
- Fast, erratic flight; often hunts over open water
- Slender to moderately robust body

### Habitat
Often found around ponds, lakes, and marshes; adults patrol open water and adjacent vegetation.

### Representative species
- Baskettails and other emerald-like genera
