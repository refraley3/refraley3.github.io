**Cordulegastridae** (spiketails) are stream-associated dragonflies with long, cylindrical abdomens and often striking yellow-and-dark patterning. They patrol stream corridors and are strong fliers.

### Field marks
- Long, cylindrical abdomen
- Contrasting yellow and dark patterning on body
- Strong association with flowing water

### Habitat
Typically found along clear streams and spring-fed creeks; adults patrol channels and nearby vegetation.

### Representative species
- Spiketails (various genera such as *Cordulegaster*)
