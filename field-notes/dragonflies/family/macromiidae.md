## Macromiidae

[← Back to Macromiidae in Family Index](/field-notes/dragonflies/family#macromiidae)
{: style="text-align: center;"}

**Macromiidae** (cruisers) are fast-flying dragonflies often seen continuously cruising above water. They have long legs and a streamlined build suited to continuous flight.

### Field marks
- Continuous cruising flight above water
- Long legs and streamlined body
- Eyes often meeting dorsally

### Habitat
Common along rivers, lakes, and large streams where adults cruise over open water.

### Representative species
- Cruisers (*Macromia* spp.)
